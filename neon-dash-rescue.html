How to play

  • Move: Arrow keys or WASD.
  • Goal: Grab yellow battery cells and touch blue mini-bots.
  • Avoid: Red glitch orbs. One hit ends the run.
  • Win style: Chain pickups to keep your energy high.

Project notes

  • Single HTML file, easy to upload to GitHub.
  • No frameworks, so it is beginner friendly.
  • Includes procedural sound and responsive canvas.

Tip: rename the file or keep it as-is and publish it with GitHub Pages.
